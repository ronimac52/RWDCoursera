Here are some audio recordings on which I play and/or sing:

I play Bass and sing with a Rhythm & Blues band called "Sidewalk Boogie".

Sidewalk Boogie are a 4 piece band consisting of Bass, Drums and two Guitars. The following recording is from a live gig in the Gallery Bar of the Ardhowen Theatre Enniskillen.

For this gig we were joined on stage by Jeannie Munroe(Piano/Keyboard) and Geoff Ward(Sax & Clarinet). The song is an original composition of mine.

I also play Double Bass and sing with a band called "Below The Line".

"Below The Line" are esentially a jazz band but we try and mix it up a litte. The band have a core of Bass, Drums & Violin, with a number of different Guitarists joining us on occasion.

The next track is a rough recording of the tune "Take 5" which was recorded at a rehearsal.

I sometimes put a band together called "The Pickups". The line-up can be different for every gig but has always included myself on Bass/Double Bass/ Vocals and Jim Bates on Drums/Vocals.

Here is a video of "The Pickups" playing a cover of "Southern Man" by Neil Young. The singer: Maurice P Collins.

Jim Bates is a prolific songwriter. He has a lot of his own work available on his YouTube channel. Check him out.



More recently I have been playing in a trio called "TroubleBass". Unfortunately we have no footage or recordings available yet.
