Coding

In the 6 months leading up to the millennium I was working for a local radio station which was based beside a community college. When I needed answers to any IT related problems I would call into the college and pick the tutors' brains. After a short while I enrolled on one of their courses The European Computer Driving Licence (ECDL) and that was me hooked on computers.

I followed that up with a number of other courses and ended up with an A+ Computer and Network Technician Certification and a JEB Teacher Trainer Diploma in IT and Adult Education. I was then employed as a teacher by them for a few years. Sadly,the college no longer exists.

My hunger for IT knowledge however is very much alive. Head on over to my coding page to see how I'm gettin' on.
